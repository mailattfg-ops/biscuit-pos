Core Features

Designed for Fast-Paced Operations

We removed the complexity of traditional POS machines. Biscuit gives you everything to run your store in a layout designed specifically for one-handed mobile use.

Visual Table Grid

Instantly see which tables are occupied, billed, or free. Color-coded borders keep your staff coordinated during rush hours.

Quick Order Queue

Easily split orders, manage parcel versus dine-in status, and print bills. Fully compatible with portable Bluetooth receipt printers.

Live Revenue Reports

Track daily orders, total sales revenue, and weekly/monthly performance targets straight from the app dashboard.

Dynamic Menu Setup

Add products, configure categories, assign pricing structures, and attach product graphics locally in just a few taps.

Flexible Configurations

Modify restaurant details, tax headers, contact numbers, table quantity limits, and manage manual backups offline.

Privacy & Security First

Your Business Data Remains Yours. Period.

BiscuitPOS is built offline-first. Your menus, billing lists, revenue logs, and client databases are kept locally on your device.

  • No internet connection required for billing or table operations.
  • No external server sync unless you explicitly trigger backups.
  • 100% compliant with standard store privacy policies.
