Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Welcome to BiscuitPOS ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of your restaurant or bakery business data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle information when you install and use the BiscuitPOS mobile application (the "App") on iOS and Android devices.

1. Information Collection and Storage

BiscuitPOS is designed as an offline-first, localized application. This means:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not require you to create an account, register, or provide your email address to use the core features of the App.
  • Local Storage: All data related to your store operations—including table numbers, order logs, menu prices, item lists, weekly/monthly sales metrics, and total revenue calculations—is stored exclusively on your device's internal local database (e.g., SQLite, CoreData, or SharedPreferences).
  • No Automatic Sync: Your business records are not automatically transmitted, synced, or backed up to any cloud service or external server managed by us.

2. Device Permissions

To deliver a complete point-of-sale experience, the App may request access to the following device hardware or software features. You can enable or disable these at any time in your system settings:

  • Bluetooth & Nearby Devices: Required to search for and connect to external thermal receipt printers (to print customer bills).
  • Local Storage / Files Access: Required if you choose to manually export backup files or CSV reports of your monthly revenue data.
  • Camera (Optional): Required only if scan-to-add features are utilized for menu barcode recognition.

3. Third-Party Services

Since the App runs fully offline, it does not share data with third-party tracking networks, advertisers, or software-as-a-service (SaaS) providers. If you download the App via the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, those platforms may compile anonymous installation statistics governed by their own privacy settings.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Because your data resides solely on your device, deleting the App will permanently remove all store records, item databases, and historical receipts. We recommend exporting CSV or PDF reports regularly for your accounting systems before uninstalling the App.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and reflected in the updated revision date. Your continued use of the App following updates signifies your acceptance of those terms.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@biscuit.pos
